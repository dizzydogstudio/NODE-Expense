## Quick Start Guide
1. Enable Notification when prompted
2. Click "+" at top right corner of the screen to add a new Tag or Group
3. After a Tag is created, click on the QR code icon on the left of the tag name to open up the QR code.
4. Use another device to scan the QR code, or share the link to a computer
5. Whoever scans the QR code can click the URL in his/her phone screen to start chatting with you anonymously.
6. Once the URL is open on the scanner's brower, your phone with this app installed will immediately get a notification.
7. If the scanner sends you a message thru the browser, you will see a badge on your tag indicating new message, and you can start chatting with the scanner.

## FAQ

**How do I create a group?**
Open the app and tap the + button on the top right corner of the screen → New Group.

**How do I create a tag?**
Open the app and tap the + button on the top right corner of the screen → New Tag.

**How do I create a tag and assign it to a group?**
Open the app and tap the + button on the top right corner of the screen → New Tag → select a group.

**How do I assign an exasting tag to a group?**
Open the group → swipe left on a tag → select "Move" to move a tag to a group.
