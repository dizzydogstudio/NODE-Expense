## FAQ

**How do I create a group?**
Open the app and tap the + button on the top right corner of the screen → New Group.

**How do I create a tag?**
Open the app and tap the + button on the top right corner of the screen → New Tag.

**How do I create a tag and assign it to a group?**
Open the app and tap the + button on the top right corner of the screen → New Tag → select a group.

**How do I assign an exasting tag to a group?**
Open the group → swipe left on a tag → select "Move" to move a tag to a group.
