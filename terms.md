## 2. Description of Service  
Node Expense: Track Together is an expense tracking app that allows you to:

- Track individual personal expenses
- Track and split group expenses with other members in real time
- Optionally record your location with expenses
- Sync expenses with selected iOS calendars
- Back up and optionally encrypt your data (PRO subscription feature)
- Export and import expense data in JSON format (PRO subscription feature)

Group-related data is synced to Firebase so all group members can view the same information in real time. Personal expense data remains on your device unless you choose to export or back it up.

## 4. Group Features & Deletion
Group expenses are synced in real time across all members' devices via Firebase. By participating in a group, you understand that:

- Your display name and expense data are visible to all members of the group
- Any member can add expenses to the group (subject to permissions set by the group admin)
- Group deletion requires unanimous consent from all members within a 7-day voting window
- Groups inactive for over 3 months may be deleted automatically if a deletion request is submitted and no member declines within 7 days
- The group admin may remove members or transfer data at any time
- Expenses you choose to keep after group deletion remain on your device locally, marked as from a deleted group
